Horse

One day where I were cleaning my computer I noticed that my background was a really boring, black box with a few white stripes running across it. I thought that I could make something more fun suiting my style, so I went on to google and searched on some different words until I saw a horse. I’m not a horse-person, and I have never ridden on a horse in my life, but I thought it had this special feeling to it, so much power. In that moment I knew I needed to make something from it that could show what I saw. This was the beginning of me making images in this artform that I love.

Koala

After some time with the horse as a background I wanted a new one, so I went to google once again to find another animal that I could make an image from. When I saw the koala, I thought it was perfect. I love the way this animal doesn’t care about anything and just happily sits on a branch eating leaves. I one again tried to capture what I saw in this animal with colours and lines.

Spotify

I love music. It contains so many emotions and can change the mood of any situation. That’s why I love using Spotify to listen to music – not only can I hear all the songs that I love but I can also listen to what my friends are listening to and find new songs. I spend a lot of my time making playlists that are harmonic or get me in a special mood. That’s why I also take the extra step and make my covers for them so I myself and others can see what to expect from the playlists.

YouTube caroline keller

I find that YouTube has become one of the biggest platforms when it comes to social media, communication and influencing in general. Therefore, I’ve also chosen to start creating thumbnails and banners for youtubers since it’s such an important part of marketing yourself and your channel. I was assigned to design the banner on the channel “Caroline Keller”. Furthermore, I also designed some of the thumbnails for the channel’s videos. The challenging part was mainly that the channel is a beauty channel. I’m personally not that familiar with the beauty industry on YouTube and therefore I had to do some research and, in cooperation with the channel, create designs that fitted the industry but at the same time also were different and eye-catching.
