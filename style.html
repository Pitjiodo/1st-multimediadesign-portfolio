What is my style?

Some of my work is very simple...
One of my favourite lines is "less is more" which you can see in some of my work. I’m very inspired by art like Picasso's "Dog".

But as sleek as one part of me is, as childish is another part. I have recently fallen in love with a more Cartoonish and graphic style. I love how diverse these styles can be, especially the cartoonish one. I can use them for not only logos and backgrounds but also for animations and websites.
