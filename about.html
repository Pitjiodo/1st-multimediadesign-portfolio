Peter Gellert Kätow

My name is Peter Gellert Kätow, I’m 20 years old and live together with my girlfriend in H.C. Andersen’s city Odense in Denmark. I’m a multimedia design student, that loves every aspect of design and loves to play and listen to music.

Ever since I were very young, I have always loved to draw, being creative and to play around with technology. It has always been a little dream of mine to make a living of drawing, designing stuff and work with computers. Now I’m studying multimedia design and wish to work with many different aspects of design and multimedia production.

I’m always trying to challenge myself, to get more knowledge and to develop my skills. That’s why I always ask questions about everything. “Everything starts with a question! Do you dare to ask?”
