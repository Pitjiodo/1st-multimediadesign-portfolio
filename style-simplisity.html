What is "Simplisity"?

When I’m making web designs I tend to use a very simple layout combined with one of the other styles I use. Take this site as an example; I try to keep it as simple as possible but still featuring my work everywhere on the website.

Sometimes I also use this style to make outlines of people. I think that it gives the person a mysterious expression that I really like.
